A grouped bar chart comparing house feature counts (bathrooms, bedrooms, floors) across different house ages (4–15 years). Consistently, bedrooms score highest, suggesting new builds prioritize bedroom count over bathroom density and multi-floor designs.

About the
Project

Developed for ABC Company as part of a Data Analytics with Tableau program on SkillWallet, this project addresses the challenge of understanding housing market dynamics. By combining thorough data preparation with impactful Tableau visualizations, the team delivers actionable insights for real estate analysts, marketing teams, and executives.

The analysis leverages a dataset of 21,609 transformed housing records, exploring renovation impact, pricing strategies, and feature-based market segmentation.
